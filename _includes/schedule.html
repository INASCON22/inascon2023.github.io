This years’ INASCON will feature inspiring plenary talks as well as thematically focused sessions. The main topics will be Intelligent Storage Materials, Photo- & Electrocatalysis, Photovoltaics & Optoelectronics, and Nano-Biophysics. In the sessions you will hear talks from leaders of the field and other young Nanoscience researchers from all over Europe. The poster sessions will allow every participant to present their work and connect. Apart from the scientific program, there will be social activities, workshops, a thought provoking plenary discussion and an opportunity fair, where you can meet companies from the greater Munich area. You can find a preliminary schedule below. Don’t forget to submit your abstract for an oral or poster contribution (click here).

On the second afternoon of our conference, various activities are planned to connect and exchange ideas in a casual atmosphere. We offer you

  • A visit of one of the largest lasers of Europe at the Centre for Advanced Laser Applications (CALA).
  • A visit of the research neutron source Heinz Maier-Leibnitz (FRM II).
  • A guided tour through the Deutsches Museum, focussing on energy technologies.
  • A workshop about “Visual Communication of Science”.
  • A workshop about “Rhetoric Extreme”. (only in german)
